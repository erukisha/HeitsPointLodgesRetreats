Meals, Amenities, and Booking Policies

Meals are available for groups of at least 10 people and can be purchased up to 2 weeks in advance. Amenities use is available for families for $20, retreats up to 50 people for $100, and groups over 50 for $200.

A signed contract and 25% deposit are required to hold your space at Heit’s Point.

Booking Information

For reservations of 6 or more hotel rooms or for any bunkhouse booking, payments become non refundable within 6 months of your scheduled stay.

We are happy to help your group find the best lodging, amenities, and meal options for your time at Heit’s Point.
